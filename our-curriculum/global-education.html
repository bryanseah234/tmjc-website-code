Global Education

With the world becoming increasingly interconnected, it is important for our students to broaden their learning and experiences to beyond our borders. The Global Education programme offers rich opportunities for students to immerse in foreign cultures, learn about emerging economies in the region, gain insights into unique societal issues, as well as contribute their services in overseas communities. Through these overseas experiences, we aim to grow our students as individuals and citizens who are equipped to be active global citizens.
